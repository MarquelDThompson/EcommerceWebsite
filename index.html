MadeItLegendary is an anonymous A.I artist based in the Bay Area. His work primarily consists of art pieces that he has created in collaboration with various Artificial Intelligences and software. His work is highly influenced by anime, manga, and pop culture with themes of Science and Science Fiction that run throughout most of his pieces. Some of his obvious inspirations come from the Mobile Suit Gundam series, Akira the movie, Berserk the Manga, and Studio Ghibli Films like Princess Mononoke.

On this website you have the ability to purchase autographed prints of his original work. Prints come in various dimensions to suit your specification needs, framed and ready to hang. All products are printed on the highest quality materials, hand signed by MadeItLegendary himself. These art works are created using cutting techniques and software that bridge the gap between the possible and impossible. What happens when the creativity of mankind and the power of technology collide? Legends are made.
